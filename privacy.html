1. Introduction

TechSphere App ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PayFlex mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use PayFlex.

This policy is prepared in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, issued by the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA).

By using PayFlex, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Data Controller TechSphere App is the Data Controller responsible for your personal data collected through the PayFlex platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: First name, last name, email address, phone number, password
  • Identity documents: National Identification Number (NIN), Bank Verification Number (BVN) when you choose to verify your identity
  • Profile photo: If you choose to upload one
  • Transaction data: Service type, recipient phone/meter/smartcard numbers, amounts, and transaction references
  • Communications: Messages and information you send to our support team

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifier
  • Usage data: Features used, screens viewed, tap interactions, session duration
  • Transaction logs: Timestamps, IP addresses, transaction statuses
  • Network information: Mobile network provider (for airtime and data purchases)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • KYC verification results: Name, date of birth, gender, and address data returned from NIMC (NIN verification)
  • Payment data: Transaction status and reference data from our payment processor (Korapay)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing Our Services

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing payments and transactions
  • Delivering purchased services (airtime, data, bills, etc.)
  • Funding and managing your Wallet

3.2 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Verifying your identity and preventing fraudulent activity
  • Detecting and investigating suspicious transactions
  • Complying with Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements
  • Recognising trusted devices and flagging unauthorised logins

3.3 Improving Our Services

  • Analysing usage patterns to improve app performance and user experience
  • Debugging errors and resolving technical issues
  • Developing new features and services

3.4 Communication

  • Sending transaction confirmations and notifications
  • Responding to support requests and complaints
  • Notifying you of important changes to our services or policies
  • Sending promotional communications (only with your consent)

3.5 Legal and Regulatory Compliance

  • Complying with applicable Nigerian laws and regulations
  • Responding to lawful requests from government authorities
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 To Deliver Services

We share necessary transaction data with third-party service providers to fulfil your purchases (e.g., your phone number with a telecom provider to process an airtime purchase).

4.2 With Payment Processors

Your payment details are processed securely by our payment partner Korapay. We share only the minimum information necessary to process your payment.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of PayFlex, our users, or the public.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

PayFlex integrates with the following categories of third-party providers to deliver our services. Each provider is bound by their own privacy policies and data protection obligations:

  • Payment processing: Korapay — for wallet top-up via card payments
  • Bill payment infrastructure: VTPass — for airtime, data, TV, and electricity services
  • VTU services: VTU Africa — for exam pins, betting top-ups, and airtime-to-cash
  • SMS/OTP delivery: Sendchamp — for verification codes and alerts
  • Identity verification: NIMC-licensed API provider — for NIN/BVN lookups
  • Business registration: CAC VAS (vasapp.cac.gov.ng) — for CAC business services
  • Cloud storage: Cloudinary — for profile photo storage

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers, as their practices are outside our direct control.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, or disclosure:

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Passwords and PINs are hashed using bcrypt — we never store them in plain text
  • Sensitive fields (NIN, BVN) are encrypted at rest in our database
  • API keys and credentials are stored as environment variables, never in source code
  • Multi-factor authentication (OTP) is required for new device logins
  • Transaction PIN is required to authorise financial transactions
  • Regular security assessments are conducted on our infrastructure
Security Incident If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to support@techsphereapp.com. Do not exploit any vulnerability you discover.

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with our legal obligations:

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account and up to 7 years after closure, as required by Nigerian financial regulations
  • Transaction records: Retained for a minimum of 5 years in compliance with AML regulations
  • KYC/Identity data: Retained for the duration of your account and up to 7 years after closure
  • Support communications: Retained for up to 2 years
  • Usage and analytics data: Retained for up to 2 years in anonymised form

When your data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised in accordance with our internal data destruction policy.

8. Your Rights Under the NDPR

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Right to Object: You may object to processing of your data for certain purposes (e.g., marketing)
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You may file a complaint with the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@techsphereapp.com. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request. Some rights may be limited where we have a legal obligation to retain data.

9. Children's Privacy

PayFlex is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete that information and close the account.

If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@techsphereapp.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you through the PayFlex app or via email at least 7 days before changes take effect

Your continued use of PayFlex after the effective date of the updated Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.
